Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

FYNX Pro (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides trading tools and utilities. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Contact info (optional): email you provide for support.
  • Purchase validation: subscription status and receipts handled by Apple to enable your Pro features.
  • Diagnostics (optional): basic crash/performance data to improve stability. No advertising identifiers.

How we use data

  • Provide and maintain app functionality.
  • Validate purchases and manage access to Pro features.
  • Improve stability and fix bugs.

Processing & storage

Most data remains on your device. Limited purchase/diagnostics data is processed by Apple and service providers we use to operate the app.

Your choices

  • Disable diagnostics in iOS Settings if you prefer.
  • You can request deletion of support emails you’ve sent us at support@elhamamini.cc.

Children

FYNX Pro is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us to remove it.

Contact

Email: support@elhamamini.cc

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
