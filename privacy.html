Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

FYNX (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides trading utilities on iOS. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data we collect

  • Purchase validation — Apple provides receipt data and subscription status to enable Pro features. We never receive full payment details.
  • Crash & performance diagnostics (optional) — Technical data such as device model, OS version, app version, and crash logs to improve stability. No advertising identifiers.
  • Support correspondence (optional) — If you email us, we receive your email address and message contents.

How we use data

  • Operate and unlock core features (e.g., validate active subscriptions).
  • Maintain reliability, performance, and security.
  • Respond to support requests.

Where data is processed

Most data remains on your device. Purchase information and diagnostics may be processed by Apple and trusted service providers solely to run the app. We do not sell or rent your data.

Your choices

  • Manage purchases and subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • Disable diagnostics if you prefer (iOS Settings or in-app toggles, where available).
  • Request deletion of support emails you’ve sent us: support@elhamamini.cc.

Children

FYNX is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child contacted us, email us to remove the information.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the “Effective date” above and post changes here.
